🌟 Project Overview
As a Student Digital Ambassador, my goal is to help my peers safely and effectively navigate the digital world. This repository serves as a master portfolio documenting my work across five distinct modules, ranging from creating professional online profiles to understanding cyber safety. Below, you will find summaries of the tasks I completed to build this resource.
📚 Module Summaries
Module 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic For this module, I designed an interactive, map-like presentation using Prezi to visually explain the core concepts of digital literacy. It highlights the importance of global connectivity, safe internet practices, and critical thinking for student success.
Module 2: Building a Student Digital Portfolio A professional digital presence is essential. In this task, I established my foundational professional footprint across three major platforms:
GitHub: To host my coding projects and version control history.
LinkedIn: To build my professional network and search for future internships.
Stack Overflow: To troubleshoot Python/ML challenges and engage with the developer community.
Module 3: Coding & Collaboration Platforms To better guide my peers in using online study tools, I explored both coding and collaborative environments. I completed the beginner "Solve Me First" challenge on HackerRank and utilized Google Workspace to build a 5-question 'Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz' using Google Forms.
Module 4: Professional Email & Etiquette Guide Effective communication is key to academic and professional success. For this module, I drafted two formal emails—one requesting an assignment extension from a professor, and another inquiring about a summer internship. Additionally, I compiled a practical "Do's and Don'ts" checklist for responsible college social media use.
Module 5: Cybercrime Awareness & Prevention Online threats are a reality, and students are often targeted. I researched and wrote a case study on a common UPI payment fraud scenario on platforms like OLX. To accompany this, I created an interactive prevention checklist tailored to college students, emphasizing secure passwords, avoiding public Wi-Fi for banking, and safe UPI practices.

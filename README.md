### 🌟 Project Overview
The goal of this repository is to showcase foundational digital skills across five distinct modules. As a Student Digital Ambassador, my objective is to guide my batchmates in creating professional online profiles, communicating responsibly, and understanding crucial cyber safety practices. 

Below is a summary of the tasks completed to build this digital portfolio.

---

### 📚 Module Summaries

**Module 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic**
For this module, I created an interactive infographic presentation using Prezi to visually explain the core concepts of digital literacy. It covers topics like global connectivity, safe internet practices, and the importance of critical thinking for student success. 

**Module 2: Building Your Student Digital Portfolio**
A professional digital presence is essential for modern students. In this task, I established my foundational footprint across three major platforms: 
* **GitHub:** To host practical projects and demonstrate my understanding of data analysis.
* **LinkedIn:** To build my professional network and search for internships.
* **Stack Overflow:** To troubleshoot coding challenges and engage with the developer community.

**Module 3: Explore Coding & Collaboration Platforms**
To better guide my peers in using online study tools, I explored both coding and collaborative environments. I completed the beginner "Solve Me First" challenge on HackerRank and utilized Google Workspace to build a 5-question 'Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz' using Google Forms.

**Module 4: Draft a Professional Email & Etiquette Guide**
Effective communication is key to academic and professional success. For this module, I drafted two formal emails—one requesting an assignment extension from a professor, and another inquiring about a summer internship. Additionally, I compiled a practical "Do's and Don'ts" checklist for responsible social media use as a college student.

**Module 5: Cybercrime Awareness Case Study & Prevention Guide**
Online threats are a reality, and students are often targeted. I researched and wrote a case study on a common UPI payment fraud scenario on resale platforms. To accompany this, I created an actionable prevention checklist tailored to college students, emphasizing secure passwords, 2FA, and safe UPI practices.
